# Client Beta: Sales Methodology Audit

**Date:** 2026-03-25
**Participants:** Xena Githubber (Consultant), Priya Sharma (VP Sales, Beta Corp)

## Context

Xena ran the diagnostic phase for Beta Corp, a SaaS company whose sales methodology is undocumented.

Priya: "Our top performer closes 3x what everyone else does, but she can't explain why. When we hired five new reps last quarter, onboarding took 4 months instead of the target 6 weeks."

## Findings

- **Top performer gap**: 3x close rate vs. team average
- **Onboarding failure**: 4 months actual vs. 6 week target
- **Root cause**: Sales methodology exists as tacit knowledge in one person
- **Recommendation**: Extract top performer's decision framework as structured playbook

Xena: "Beta Corp's entire revenue depends on one person's intuition. That's not a sales team, it's a single point of failure. We need to make Priya's star's methodology explicit and teachable."
