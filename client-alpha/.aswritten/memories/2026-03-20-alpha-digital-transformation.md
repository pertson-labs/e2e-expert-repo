# Client Alpha: Digital Transformation Assessment

**Date:** 2026-03-20
**Participants:** Xena Githubber (Consultant), Marcus Webb (CTO, Alpha Industries)

## Context

Xena ran the diagnostic phase for Alpha Industries, a manufacturing company transitioning to digital-first operations.

Marcus: "We've been running on spreadsheets and tribal knowledge for 20 years. When our senior engineers retire, their process knowledge walks out the door. Last year we lost three experts and it took 6 months to recover."

## Findings

- **Knowledge loss rate**: 3 senior experts departed in 12 months
- **Recovery time**: 6 months per expert departure
- **Critical gap**: Manufacturing process parameters stored in personal notebooks, not systems
- **Recommendation**: Capture process parameters as structured knowledge before next retirement wave

Xena: "Alpha Industries is a textbook case. The knowledge exists but it's trapped in people's heads. We need to extract it before the next retirement wave hits in Q4."
