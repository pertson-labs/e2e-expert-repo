# Xena's Consulting Methodology

**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Participants:** Xena Githubber (Founder, Pertson Labs)

## Context

Xena documented her consulting methodology after 5 years of independent practice.

Xena: "Every engagement starts with a diagnostic phase. I spend two weeks interviewing stakeholders, mapping their decision-making process, and identifying where institutional knowledge is being lost. The diagnostic alone is worth $5K — most clients have never seen their own knowledge gaps mapped."

Key principles:
- **Diagnostic first**: Never propose solutions before understanding the system
- **Knowledge mapping**: Visualize where decisions happen and where knowledge lives
- **Stakeholder interviews**: Talk to at least 8 people across 3 levels
- **Deliverable**: A knowledge gap report with prioritized recommendations
